Warren Buffett

Man who achieved greatness

Here is the time line for Buffett's life

  • 1930 - Warren Edward Buffett is born on August 30,1930 in Omaha, Nebraska as the second of three children to Howard and Leila Buffett, who married in 1925
  • 1942 - After predicting to a childhood friend he will be a millionaire by the time he is 35, Buffett buys his first stock at the age of 11
  • 1943 - Buffett family moves to Washington
  • 1949 - Discovers “The Intelligent Investor”
  • 1952 - Marries Susan Thompson
  • 1956 - Starts first Buffett Partnership
  • 1958 - Buys modest Omaha house
  • 1959 - Meets Charlie Munger
  • 1965 - Buys Berkshire Hathaway
  • 1972 - Berkshire acquires See’s Candies
  • 1983 - In May, the price of one share of Berkshire Hathaway stock tops $1000 for the first time
  • 1988 - Buys Coca-Cola stake
  • 1991 - Meets Bill Gates
  • 1992 - In November, the price of one share of Berkshire Hathaway stock tops $10,000 for the first time
  • 1998 - In January, the price of one share of Berkshire Hathaway stock tops $50,000 for the first time
  • 1999 - At height of internet bubble, Buffett warns investors are “expecting far too much”
  • 2006 - Teams with Gates Foundation to give away almost all his wealth
  • 2006 - Marries Astrid Menks
  • 2006 - In October, the price of one share of Berkshire Hathaway stock tops $100,000 for the first time
  • 2008 - Writes “Buy American. I am” op-ed for the NY Times
  • 2011 - Receives Presidential Medal of Freedom
  • 2012 - On April 17, Buffett reveals he has been diagnosed with stage I prostate cancer
  • 2015 - In August, the price of one share of Berkshire Hathaway stock tops $200,000 for the first tim
  • 2017 - In December, the price of one share of Berkshire Hathaway stock tops $300,000 for the first time
